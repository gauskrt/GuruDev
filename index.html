About me

Gurudev draws from the edges of perception — where the familiarity folds. Space, ink, contrast are his language. Each piece is a different way of seeing.

My Inspiration

If not us
- then who?

It is of my understanding that everything happens in chance. We are just the universe experincing itself. My work explores that charged void: ink split onto paper, colour to build contrast that only we understand. Abstract not as abstraction — but to establish a different kind of seeing.
