About me

I draw from the edges of perception — where the familiar folds into the cosmic. Space, ink, and abstract geometries are my language. Each piece is a different way of seeing the same impossible thing.

My Inspiration & Style

Space is not
empty —

It is full of tension, mystery, and impossible geometry. My work explores that charged void: ink pressed into paper like starlight pressing through dark matter. Forms that shouldn't exist but insist on being drawn. Abstract not as abstraction — but as a different kind of seeing.
